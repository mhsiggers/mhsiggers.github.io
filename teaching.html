Did you enjoy a class that you took, and think you would like to see more of it? Do you wonder what math 'research' is? Do you like drawing triangles?

Most of the professors in the department love it when a student shows interest, and would like to help you learn more about math.

I do graph theory. Mostly we just draw triangles. If you would like to see what it is about, please feel free to come by and learn about it.
