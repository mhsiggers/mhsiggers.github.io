Syllabus

Some familiarity with graphs is expected, We expect to cover chapters 5-18 of the text, spending about a week each on the following topics.
  • Matchings and Systems of Distinct representatives
  • Extremal set theory
  • Network flows
  • DeBruijn Sequences
  • Associative Block Designs
  • Inclusion-exclusion, Mobius inversion
  • Permanents
  • van der Waerden's Theorem
  • Stirling Numbers
  • Recursion and generating functions
  • Partitions
  • Latin Squares
  • Reed Muller Codes

Grading

Grading will be discussed in class, but: students WILL be asked to prepare and present topics, and there WILL be at least one test.
