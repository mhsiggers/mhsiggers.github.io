Syllabus

We will stay pretty close to the following schedule. The specific sections in the text that I intend to cover are listed on the homework page.
Week Topics
1 Fundamentals of counting, permutations, combinations
2 - 3 Formal logic: truth tables, logical equivalence and inference, proof by contradiction, quantification
4 Set theory: basic laws, Venn Diagrams, probability
5 - 6 Induction and recursion.
7 Generating Functions
8 Midterm Exam
9 Recurrance relations
10 - 11 Graph Theory
12 Trees
13 Matchings
14 Final Exam
