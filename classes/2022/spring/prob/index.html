Syllabus

We will cover chapters 1 through 4 of the text, and may add some applications to random graphs. The mathematical objectives are:
  • Understand probability spaces and discrete and continuous random variables
  • Computr basic expectations such as mean varience and moment generating functions.
  • Transform multivariate distributions and find their marginal and conditional distributions.
  • Become familiar with basic distributions; in particular poisson and normal distributions.
  • Understand what a random experiment is, and be able to compute confidence intervals and set up a hypothesis test.
