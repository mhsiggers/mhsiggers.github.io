Syllabus

We will introduce Algebraic Topology following Hatcher [Ha], emphasising the algebraic calculation of invariants over a geometric intuition. We will mostly skip Chapter 0 of [Ha] and quickly cover Chapter 1. We then cover Chapters 2 and the first half of Chapter 3 in more detail, giving stronger emphasis to categorical language than the text does. Our main alternate reference will be Wikipedia [Wiki] . We will have three homework assignments, and a final test. Each of these four items will be worth %25 of your grade.

Homework

There will be suggested homework problems for each class. The material quickly gets abstract, and you will not learn it unless you do several of these. On March 31, April 28, and May 25 (all thursdays), I will choose some of the problems that have been suggested upto that time, and you will hand them in by the following Tuesday.

As much as possible, I will grade separately for correctness, and for writing/technique. You will have the opportunity to resubmit a homework to improve you writing mark.

Tests

The test will be on Thursday June 9. If there is a problem with this, let me know before April. The test will draw mostly, but not entirely, from suggested problems.
