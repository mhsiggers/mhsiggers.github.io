Syllabus

We will stay pretty close to the following schedule. The specific sections in the text that I intend to cover are listed on the homework page.
Week Topics
1 Fundamentals of counting, permutations, combinations
2 - 3 Formal logic: truth tables, logical equivalence and inference, proof by contradiction, quantification
4 Set theory: basic laws, Venn Diagrams, probability
5 - 6 Induction and recursion.
7 Generating Functions
8 Midterm Exam
9 Recurrance relations
10 - 11 Graph Theory
12 Trees
13 Matchings
14 Final Exam

Homework

There will be four required homework assignments. On top of this I will list 'suggested problems'. These are problems that you should at least look at and be able to do. Your test problems will be made up largely from these or similar problems. If you you would like input on your solutions to these problems you may hand them in.
