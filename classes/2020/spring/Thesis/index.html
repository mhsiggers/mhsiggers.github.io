Syllabus

Every week, this semester, I will meet one-on-one with each student weekly for an hour. If the student does not yet have a thesis topic, they will be given readings, and in the hour we meet we will discuss these readings. Generally this discussion starts with you presenting the readings to me, and asking any questions you have about them. The goal of these reading is to get you to learn the background of your eventual thesis topic, but also to practice reading and presenting math research. Reading research mathematics often entails searching out supporting material for context and definitions. Presenting it calls for you to learn how to decide what is important and novel in a paper, and what is standard and background.

Grading

Grading is on an S/U basis, and is very subjective, but I will give you frequent feedback about how you are doing.
