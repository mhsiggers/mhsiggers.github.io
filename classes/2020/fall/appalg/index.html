Syllabus

In the first four weeks of Applied Algebra, we will look at the application of Universal Algebra to the complexity analysis of constraint satisfaction problems.
  • Graph Homomorphisms and gadget constructions
  • From Graphs to Constraint Satisfaction Problems: SAT, Digraphs, Reflexive Graphs, and List-Hom
  • Universal algebra: pp-formula, clones and varieties
  • Three proofs of the H-colouring dichotomy
