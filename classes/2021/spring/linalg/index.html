Syllabus

We will cover the first four chapters of the text, and possibly some of the fifth.
We will mostly follow the order in the text.
Here is an approximate schedule.

Week Sections Topics
1 1.1 - 1.3 Gaussian Elimination
2 1.4, 1.6 Matrices
3 1.5, 2.1 Triangle Factorisation, Vector spaces
4 2.2 - 2.3 Linear Independence
5 2.4 - 2.5 Rowspace, Networks
6 2.6 Linear Transformations
7 - Test 1
8 3.1 - 3.2 Subspaces, Cosines
9 3.3 Projections
10 3.4 - 3.5 Gram-Schmidt
11 4.1 - 4.2 Determinants
12 4.3 Determinant Formulas
13 4.4 Applications
14 - Test 2
