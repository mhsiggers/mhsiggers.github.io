Syllabus

In this class we look at basic ideas of differential equations, linear algebra, and numerical analysis that are useful in Chemistry. We will cover the following topics from the text.
Week Sections Topics
1 7.1 - 7.2 Matrices
2 7.3 - 7.4 Gaussian Elimination, Rank, Vector Spaces
3 7.7 - 7.8 Determinants, Cramer's Rule, Inverses
4 8.1 - 8.3 Eigenvalues, Symmetric and Orthogonal Matrices
5 8.4, 1.1 Quadratic Forms, Differential Equations
6 1.2-1.3 Eulers Method, Separable ODE's
7 1.4, 1.5, 2.1 Integrating Factors, Bernoulli Equation, Second order Linear ODEs
8 2.1 - 2.2 Homogeneous Linear ODEs with constant coefficients
9 2.4 - 2.5 Modelling free oscillations, Euler-Cauchy equations
10 2.6, 2.7, 2.10 Wronskians, Non-homogeneous ODEs, Variation of Parameters
11 5.1 - 5.3 Power Series, Legendre Polynomials, Frobenius Method
12 6.1 - 6.2 Laplace Transforms, Transforms of Derivatives and Integrals
13 6.3, 6.4, 6.6 Second Shifting Theorem, Diracs's Delta Function, Differentiation and Integrations of Transforms
