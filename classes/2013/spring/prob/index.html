Syllabus

Over two semester's we will try to cover most of the course text, being more thorough in the first half of the text. The text is difficult in places, so we may use the gentler presentation in the supplementary texts for some topics.

In the first semester we should cover upto Chapters 4 or 5. The main topics this semester will include:
Probability and Random Variables; Multivariate Distrubutions; Binomial, Poisson, Normal, t and F distributions; Statistical Inference; The Central Limit Theorem

Homework

There will be homework problems assigned for each class. You don't have to do them, but the test will draw largely from them.
