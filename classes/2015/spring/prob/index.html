Syllabus

Over two semester's we will try to cover chapters 1 through 9 of the text. In the first semester we will cover chapters 1 through 4.

The main topics this semester will include:
Probability and Random Variables; Multivariate Distrubutions; Binomial, Poisson, Normal, t and F distributions; Statistical Inference; The Central Limit Theorem

Here is an approximate schedule.
