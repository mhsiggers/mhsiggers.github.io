Syllabus

We will cover substitution ciphers, discrete logarithms, Diffie-Hellman Key exchange, ElGamal and RSA and other public key cryptosystems, probabilistic encryption, the Vigenere cypher, and elliptic curve cryptography. To do this we will have to touch on the following background topics: computational complexity, modular arithmetic, finite fields, quadratic residues, probability, and elliptic curves.
