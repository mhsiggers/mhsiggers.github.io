Syllabus

This is the continuation of Math 231. We will cover Chapters 5-8 of the text.
Week Sections Topics
1 5.1 - 5.2 Eigenvalues and Diagonalization
2 5.3 Difference Equations
3 5.4 - 5.5 Differential Equations and Complex Matrices
4 5.6 Similarity Transformations
5 6.1-6.2 Maxima and Positive Definiteness
6 6.3 Singular Value Decomposition
7 - Test 1
8 6.4 - 6.5 Minimum Principles and Finite Element Method
9 7.1 - 7.2 Matrix Norms and Conditioning
10 7.3 - 7.4 Eigenvalue computation and iterative methods
11 8.1 - 8.2 Linear Programming: The Simplex Method
12 8.3 - 8.4 The dual problem
13 8.5 Game theory
14 - Test 2

Homework and Quizzes

Homework will not be assigned but suggested problems will be given.
On mondays there will often be a quiz at the start of class.
It will usually be one of the suggested problems.



Tests

There will be two tests. The first one will be on Oct 19 or 23, and the final will be Dec 11 or 14. We will decide the date of the exams at least 2 weeks before the exam. About 60% of the questions on the test will be assigned homework problems or from previous tests. 20% will be other problems from the book. 20% will be proofs or examples from class.

Evaluation

Quizzes: 10%, Tests 2 x 45%. For the quiz mark, we will have upto 10 quizzes, each will be worth 1% of the total grade. You will get at least half the marks on a quiz for a blank quiz, so this is an attendence mark as well.
