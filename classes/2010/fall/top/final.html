About the Final

Below is the schedule for the Final. I have changed some of your times, slightly, but not by more than 20 minutes. I have also changed some topics, as more than one of you chose the same topic. If the times are inconvenient, or you want to change topics, (or I have made a mistake,) please contact me soon.

Location

My office.

Format

You will present your topic for 5-10 minutes. Then I will ask questions related to your topic, or related to problems you had trouble with on the first and second test. All together this should take about 15 minutes. ( It might happen that I ask questions during your presentation, instead of after. Be ready for this. )

Tips

Aim your presentation to be about 8 minutes, and practice it. Some of the topics lend to a longer or shorter presentation, so you will likely have to omit some details. You should know any details you omit, because this is likely something I'm going to ask about. If the topic you chose doesn't give you enough material for 8 minutes, find appropriate material to add. When choosing what details to include, remember that you are trying to impress me with what you know. So don't choose the easiest 8 minutes you can, and don't talk about something that you don't understand.
