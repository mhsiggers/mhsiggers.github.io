Syllabus

In the first part of this class, we covered the first 5 chapters of the text. In this part, we will cover Chapters 6-8.
We will mostly follow the order in the text.
We will have four homework assignments, one mid-term test, and a final test. The grades will be accorded the following weights.
Attendence: 10%
Homework: 10%
Mid-term: 40%
Final: 40%

Homework

There will be four required homework assignments. On top of this I will list 'suggested problems'. These are problems that you should at least look at and be able to do. Your test problems will be made up largely from these or similar problems. If you you would like input on your solutions to these problems you may hand them in.

Tests
