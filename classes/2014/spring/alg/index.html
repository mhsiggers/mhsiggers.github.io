Seminar

Syllabus

We plan to cover Chapters 1 - 11 of the text, and time permitting, Chapter 24. This covers group theory: basic facts and definitions, cyclic and permutation groups, subgroups and cosets, normal subgroups, factor groups, homomorphisms isomorphisms, the fundamental theorem of algebra, and possibly (Chap 24) the Sylow theorems.
