Syllabus

We relearn the basics of differential and integral calculus with more mathematical rigour. Taking up where we left off in Analysis 1, we begin the class by looking at infinite series. We will consider various notions of convergence of such series and then look their use in approximating functions. This provide a starting point for carrying the concepts of the first semester, which we did exclusively over the reals, into more general spaces.

We will try to cover Chapters 6 to 10 of the text. Here is a tentative weekly schedule.
