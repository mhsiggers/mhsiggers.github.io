Syllabus

This is a continuation of Algebra I, in which I assume you have become familiar with Groups, including such things as Cosets, Homomorphsims, and group actions. We will follow the text, and continue with basic Ring and Field theory. This covers such things as polynomial and quotient rings and ideals; extension fields, the Sylow theorems, UFDs and, if you are well behaved, Galois Theory.
