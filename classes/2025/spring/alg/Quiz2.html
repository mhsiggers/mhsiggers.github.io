True or False Questions

  1. Every subgroup of every group has left and right cosets.

  2. A subgroup of a finite group has the same number of left and right cosets.

  3. Every prime order group is abelian

  4. Every group has a kernel.

  5. An infinite subgroup of an infinite group has finitely may cosets

  6. If a finite group G has order n then gn = e for every g in G

  7. Every abelian group of prime power order is cyclic.

  8. ℤ2 × ℤ4 is isomorphic to ℤ8

  9. ℤ3 × ℤ8 is isomorphic to S4

  10. Every finite abelian group has a Betti number of 0.

  11. An abelian group of order 72 can have at most two subgroup of order 4.

  12. ℤ8 is generated by {3,6}.

  13. An is a normal subgroup of Sn.

  14. Every homomorphism has a non-empty kernel.

  15. For any two groups G and H there is a homomorphism from G to H

  16. There is a non-trivial homomorphism of some group of order 6 to a group of order 12

  17. There is a non-trivial homomorphism of some group of order 6 to a group of order 10

  18. Any inner automorphism of an abelian group is the identity

  19. Every subgroup of an abelian group is normal.

  20. Every factor group of a torsion-free group is torsion-free.

  21. Every normal subgroup of a group is the kernel of some group homomorphism

  22. Every factor group of a group G is isomorphic to a normal subgroup of G.

  23. For an normal subgroup N of G with (G : N) = m we have gm ∈ N for all g ∈ G.
