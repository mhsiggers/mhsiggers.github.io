Midterm

  • For the midterm you have a choice of a test or a project/presentation.
  • If you want to do a project/presentation, email me a proposed topic by April 12. If you do not send it by this time, I expect you to write the midterm test.
  • Good topics are: more depth on something we've done, or an explanation how graph theory is applied to (prefereably your) research.
  • The presentation should be a 10 minute slide presentation. If you do a project without presentation, it should be under 3 pages.
