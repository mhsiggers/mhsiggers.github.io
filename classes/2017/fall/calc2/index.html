Syllabus

The text is in Korean, but the class will be taught entirely in English.

We cover the basic results of vector calculus.

The following is an approximate schedule of classes.

Week Sections Topics
1 7.1 Vectors
2 7.2, 7.3 Vector functions; curve length and curvature
3 8.1, 8.2 Two variable functions; limits and continuity
4 8.3 Multi-variate functions
5 8.4, 8.5 The Chain Rule; directional derivatives
6 8.6 Extremal points
7 - Test 1
8 9.1 Double integrals
9 9.2 Double integrals in polar coordinates
10 9.3 Triple integrals
11 10.1, 10.2 Line integrals; fundamental theorem and Greene's theorem
12 10.3 Surface integrals
13 10.4 Stokes theorem and the divergence theorem
14 Buffer and Review
15 - Test 2
