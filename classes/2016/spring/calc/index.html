Syllabus

The text is in Korean, but the class will be taught entirely in English.

We cover the basic techniques and application of differential and integral calculus.

The following is an approximate schedule of classes.

Week Sections Topics
1 1.1, 1.2 Functions and their limits
2 1.3, 2.1, 2.2 Continuity and the derivative
3 2.3, 3.1 Chain Rule, Mean Value Theorem
4 3.2 Extremal Values,
5 3.3, 3.4 Velocity and Acceleration, Differentials,
6 4.1, 4.2 Antiderivatives, Integrals
7 - Test 1
8 4.3, 4.4 Substitution, Integration by parts, trigonometric substititution
9 4.5, 5.1 Other integration techniques, Area and Volume
10 5.2 Arc length, surface area
11 5.3, 5.4 Polar coordinates, other applications
12 6.1, 6.2 Sequences, series
13 6.3 Series convergence tests
14 6.4, 6.5 Power series
15 - Test 2
